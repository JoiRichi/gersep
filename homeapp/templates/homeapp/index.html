    About Us

    The Graduate Employment and Research Skill Enhancement Project (GERSEP) is the brain child of the Innovative Science Research Foundation (ISREF). It was an idea conceived in the year 2013 with the intention of recruiting Graduate Interns and equipping them with intellectual skills that would help them face the challenges of life after graduating from their various Universities. ISREF is a duly registered Non-Profit/Non-Governmental Organization, with Anthonio Research Publications (ARP) and the International Society of Science Researchers (ISSCIR) as affiliates. ARP is the publisher of the International Journal of Basic, Applied and Innovative Research (IJBAIR), International Journal of Community Research (IJCR) and the International Journal of Herbs and Pharmacological Research (IJHPR), while ISSCIR is a duly registered Academic Society for Innovative Scientists.

    Mission

    The Mission of GERSEP is to help young graduates develop capacities for problem identification, problem analysis and solution generation, as well as innovative thinking, research/grant fund mining and team work ethics.

    Vision

    The vision of GERSEP is to create enabling platforms for young graduates to achieve:

    • Overall research proficiency with specialization in identified areas of interest.
    • Capacity to discern and apply learnt skills for community service.
    • Pass recommended online courses (especially in identified areas of specialization) that are offered by recognized institutions.
    • Capacity for independent innovative applications of learnt skills.
    • Publish 15 or more works in renowned online based Journals.
    • Attend academic conferences in the capacity of a Student/Interns.
    • Design and test-run a private enterprise model in your chosen area of specialization, and
    • Identify specific fields for possible application submission.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Gersep for profit making?

    No, Gersep is more concerned about investing in Graduates than making profit.

    Are Gersep certifications recognised?

    Yes, Gersep certificates are awarded on merit by the Director, a University full Professor.

    Why should I intern at Gersep?

    Gersep will definitely leave you fit to survive in the 21st century market.

    Is Gersep for a specific field?

    Gersep is currently focused on areas within Sciences and innovation.

    Is the Internship paid?

    Depending on the criteria of admission, some of the interns will be paid stipends.

    What is the duration of the Internship?

    The duration of the internship is based on the discretion of the management and is announced alongside the call for application.
